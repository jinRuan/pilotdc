Our Mission...

is to emphasize this:

HACKATHONS = LEARNING + AWESOME

High school Hackathons are valuble opportunities that provide high school students with little or no programming experience with vital experience and exposure to computer science, all wrapped up with FUN. Our mission is to promote computer science within the younger communities and expose them to the art that is computer programming.
