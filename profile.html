Why Attend Hackathons at all? Seems like a lotta work...

Hackathons are an amazing place to meet people with similar interests. Even if you are not interested in CS in general, you can still meet lifelong friends at these events.

For the people who ARE into CS, hackathons are even more important. Not only do they help create a feeling of what it is like to develop a real product in real life, they also help you work with industry professionals and work with colleageas of similar interests.
